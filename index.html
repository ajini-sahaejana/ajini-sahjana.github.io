About Us

We at YVM believes that each and every individual, irrespective of your economic status, interests, geographic location, religion or any other category you fall into can contribute immensely in making their communities little better while developing the nation as a whole. So we works tirelessly night and day to make sure your voice is heard loud and clear by the authorities and the relevant parties responsible and acted upon promptly to solve the burning issues in your local community.

Why Choose Us?

Our mission is to unite the local communities and support them to make sure that their problems are heard and taken care of while empowering them to come forward and make the necessary changes for the betterment of the society.

Platform to Speak up

Our platform is built in a way that any individual can come forward and write about an issue in their local community, work place or any other location without the fear of implications that would arise.

Platform where your voice is heard

Our platform makes sure that your message or the concern is directed towards the relevant parties including the governments, local members of the parliament, council members and other relevant authorities promptly.

Platform where actions are implemented

Our platform makes sure whenever an issue is raised, we always track it and follows it up until the necessary actions are taken to completely eliminate the problem.

Platform to empower crowd funding

Our platform enables communities to get together in harmony, raise funds through crowd funding and implement solutions to resolve the problems at hand without waiting for the authorities to take action.

KEY AREAS OF FOCUS

Your Voice Matters is platform where any one can submit any concern they have about their local communities. However, in order to make it easier for everyone we have identified several key areas of focus.

Transport

This category includes any issues related to transport which includes public transport, parking or even traffic violations etc.

Law and Order

This category focuses on the issues related to garbage management, city infrastructure and public areas.

Health and Safety

This category includes any issues that would relate to the public health and safety while minimizing any risks associated.

City Service

This category focuses on the issues related to garbage management, city infrastructure and public areas.

Education

This category includes any issue related to schools, universities and private education institutes and educational authorities.

Fair Trading

This category includes any issues related to trading scams and quality issues connected to all types of shops and supermarkets in your local community.
